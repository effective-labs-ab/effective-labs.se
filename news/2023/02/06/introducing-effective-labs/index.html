Introducing Effective Labs

2023-02-06 by Ryan
  • Effective Labs
  • Woodland WMS
  • Logistics

Established in early 2023, Effective Labs is a new player in the competitive software business aiming to be a future leader in software development. Initial efforts will be to develop user-friendly software solutions, primarily within logtech.

Logistics

Woodland WMS, the flagship product from Effective Labs, was born out of the frustration of using existing warehouse management systems lacking in flexibility and user-friendliness.

As the growth of online shopping only increases with each year passing by, the tools used in the industry must follow along. This is where Woodland WMS comes to the rescue!

Built from the start to offer a flexible and easy-to-use system, Woodland WMS can help any company of any size to optimize their logistics workflow.

More information about Woodland WMS can be found on our dedicated Woodland WMS website. It’s in Swedish, translations are coming!

Software Development

Effective Labs realizes not only the logistics industry wants user-friendly systems, so why stop there?

In addition to developing proprietary systems, Effective Labs can be your go-to-partner for anything software development related. Of course we can’t do everything, but if it’s related to Linux, C++, or .NET I’m sure we can help.
