Meet Ryan

Founder, CEO and Lead Architect of Effective Labs, Ryan is an experienced computer programmer. His coding journey began back in 1998 when he bought the book The C++ Programming Language by Bjarne Stroustrup.

Ryan, who are you?

I'm probably a typical geek who enjoys anything tech-related. I've always been fascinated by technology and I remember being more interested in Microsoft Works (spreadsheets) than games when I was a kid. After I learned programming in 1998 I never stopped.

What do you do on your free time?

On my occasional free time, I enjoy DJing, building Lego models, and taking pictures of nature/animals. You can read more about my other hobbies on my social media.
